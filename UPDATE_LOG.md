### KSBP Details
- **Airport:** San Luis Obispo County Regional Airport
- **ICAO Code:** KSBP
- **Location:** San Luis Obispo, California
- **Coordinates:** 35.2368°N, 120.6424°W
- **Distance from KBDN:** Approximately 520 nautical miles south
- **Elevation:** 212 feet MSL

### Flight Considerations (KBDN → KSBP)
- **Route:** Typically south through Central Valley or coastal route
- **Distance:** ~520 nm (about 3.5-4 hours in RV7 at cruise)
- **Terrain:** Crosses mountain ranges, recommend IFR-rated route planning
- **Weather:** Check marine layer if flying coastal route
- **Fuel:** Well within RV7 range but plan a fuel stop for safety margins
- **Common Stops:** KSAC, KMOD, or coastal airports for fuel/rest
